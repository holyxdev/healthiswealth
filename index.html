01. The Vision

Inspired by SDG 3

"Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages."

This podcast is our response to the United Nations’ call for global well-being. For university students, health is more than just the absence of illness—it’s the strength to navigate stressful exams, the energy from balanced nutrition, and the joy of meaningful social connections. Each episode dives into practical tips, inspiring stories, and expert advice, helping students build resilience, maintain mental clarity, and create a lifestyle where well-being thrives.

02. The Hosts

From UAB, With Love

The story started in the bustling Plaça Cívica at UAB. Four friends, same major, buried under assignments and fueled by caffeine, racing against deadlines. As we chased grades, we noticed something crucial slipping away—our well-being. That moment sparked an idea: why not start a conversation about health, balance, and life as a student?
